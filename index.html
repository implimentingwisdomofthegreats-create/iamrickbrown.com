About

Meet Rick Brown

Rick is the kind of person who doesn't just talk about adventure — he lives it. At 18 years old, he loaded up a touring bike and pedaled across the entire United States. That grit never faded.

Today, he's still riding, still competing on the pickleball court, and still building — literally — the roads that connect communities across the country. When he's not closing deals on heavy equipment or chasing down a winning shot, you'll find him out on the water at Falls Lake or spending time with the people who matter most — his three kids.
